Case and cooling system

Case

The case is the largest and bulkiest component of a computer.

It is made of various metals, alloys and plastics and is designed to protect the rest of the components from dust and other external factors such as physical shocks.

The cases are factory-fitted with various component mounting slots: motherboard slot, source slot, hard drive slot, optical drive slot, video card slot, additional cooling slot.


Among the popular brands of market carcasses can be counted: Aqirys, Cooler Master, Segotep, Lian Li, Corsair.

Nowadays, enclosures come in universal sizes (Full-Tower, Mid-Tower, Mini-Tower) and almost all motherboard formats can be mounted in them.


Cooling systems

For components to work properly, a personal computer must be equipped with an efficient cooling system

Most often it is resorted to mounting four fans, powered on MOLEX or 4-pin plug, in the specially provided slots of the case.


For personal computers used in games or really demanding programs and applications, consumers prefer to use liquid cooling or AIO (All In One) systems because of their efficiency.

Huge manufacturers of high-performance cooling systems include: Corsair, Noctua, Thermaltake, Aqirys, Cooler Master
