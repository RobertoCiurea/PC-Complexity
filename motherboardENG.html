Motherboard


Motherboard is an essential component of a computer, being a main integrated circuit to which the rest of the hardware components are mounted and connected.

The motherboard contains buses that allow a computer's data to flow between all hardware components.

Over the years, the market has seen a lot of motherboard brands such as Gigabyte, MSI, Intel, Asrock, each bringing a new generation and new technology and many more features.


Components of a motherboard

  • Microprocessor socket
  • RAM slots
  • Power plug
  • USB 2.0, 3.0, 3.1 plugs
  • SATA plugs
  • IDE plugs
  • BIOS batery
  • BIOS chip
  • 4 pin fan power plug
  • 4 pin power plug extra
  • Front panel plugs
  • PCI-Expres slots
  • Exit ports(USB 2.0, USB 3.0, VGA, DVI, HDMI, Audio)


Dimensions of a motherboard over time

Motherboards have evolved to many shapes and sizes.

  • Size XT 216mm x 279mmmade in 1983.


  • SizesATX (most popular and most used): eATX 305mm x 330mm ATX 305mm x 244mm. made in 1995; microATX: 244mm x 244mm made in 1997.


  • Sizes ITX: Mini-ITX 170mm x 170mm creata in 2003; Nano-ITX 120mm x 120mm made in 2004; Pico-ITX 100mm x 72mm made in 2007.
