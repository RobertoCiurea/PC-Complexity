Power supply

The power supply or power block is a vital component of a computer, which supplies all other components with the exact amount of power they need and thus ensures their operation.

Power absorbed from a high-voltage (100 - 240V) household power source is converted to low-voltage (3.3 - 12V) DC power using active and passive components through switching power supply technology, while achieving high voltage stability at various power draws from hardware components.

For a good functioning of the sources they are equipped with radiators and fans to ensure a flow of air from the external environment.

The market has seen a lot of power supply companies such as: Aqirys, Cooler Master, Lian Li, Seasonic, nJoy, Corsair, Zalman.

A high performance category of power supplies is the modular category which allows the user to detach the cables to replace them with new ones.


The connectors with which a power supply must be provided:

  • 20+4 pin motherboard connector
  • 15-pin SATA connector
  • 4-pin power connector
  • 6+2 pin power connector
  • 8-pin power connector
  • MOLEX power connectors
